Our Mission

The Mission Mode Project (MMP) of Instructure Scholarships Portal under the National e-Governance Plan aims at providing common electronic portal for implementing various Scholarships schemes launched by Central Government, State Government and Union Territories across the country.

Our Vision

Instructure Scholarship Portal is one-stop solution through which various services starting from student application, processing & providing of various scholarships to Students are enabled.
